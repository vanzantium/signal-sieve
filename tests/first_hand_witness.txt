I was present at the all-staff meeting on November 3, 2024, when the CEO announced the restructuring directly to employees via a recorded company-wide call. He stated clearly that the company would be cutting 12 percent of the global workforce by Q1 2025, effective immediately for the Toronto office. The Chief People Officer confirmed the timeline in the same session.

I spoke personally with three colleagues who received written notice that same afternoon. The recorded call lasted approximately 45 minutes. The severance package described was four weeks of base pay per year of service, capped at 26 weeks, with extended health benefits through March 31, 2025.

The announcement was not preceded by any public disclosure. No regulatory filing or press release had appeared on SEDAR or the company's investor relations page as of the time of writing.
