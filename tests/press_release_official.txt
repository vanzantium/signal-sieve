Acme Corp Announces Record Q3 2024 Revenue

TORONTO, ON — November 4, 2024 — Acme Corp (NASDAQ: ACME) today announced financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2024.

Q3 2024 Revenue: $1.2 billion, up 8% year-over-year. Net income was $142 million, or $1.14 per diluted share.

"We are pleased to report another strong quarter," said CEO Jane Doe. "Despite FX headwinds in key markets, our core business performed above expectations. We see headwinds in Q4 due to FX exposure."

The company reaffirms guidance for Q4 2024 revenue of $1.15 to $1.20 billion, subject to exchange rate movements and macro conditions.
